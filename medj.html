TOP 10 MEDICAL COLLEGES IN NORTH INDIA

  1. AIIMS

    The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, has control over the autonomous collection of public medical universities known as the All India Institutes of Medical Sciences (AIIMS). These institutions have been designated as Institutes of National Importance by a Parliamentary Act. The pioneering institution, AIIMS New Delhi, was founded in 1956. 24 additional institutes have since been announced. Twenty institutes are up and running as of January 2023, while four more shouldn't start up until 2025. There were proposals for six more AIIMS. It is regarded as South Asia's first medical institution.

    Official website

    ELIGIBILITY:
    AIIMS MBBS Eligibility Criteria are the sets of criteria to be fulfilled to register for NEET UG entrance exam and take admission in AIIMS MBBS course.

  2. LADY HARDINGE MEDICAL COLLEGE

    In New Delhi, India, there is a medical school for women called Lady Hardinge Medical College. It was founded in 1916 and joined the University of Delhi's Faculty of Medical Sciences in 1950. The Government of India provides funding for the college. Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Applicants are selected on the based on NEET UG,PG scores.

  3. MAULANA AZAD MEDICAL COLLEGES (MAMC)

    The Delhi government oversees Maulana Azad Medical College (MAMC), a medical school in New Delhi, India that is connected with the University of Delhi. It bears Maulana Abul Kalam Azad's name, an Indian freedom fighter and the country's first minister of education following independence. In the vicinity of Delhi Gate on Bahadur Shah Zafar Marg, it was founded in 1959. With a combined bed capacity of 2800 among four hospitals connected to MAMC, millions of people in Delhi alone and many more from the neighbouring states in north India are served. The college offers teaching programmes for graduate and postgraduate degrees, residencies, and subspecialties/fellowships (sometimes known as superspecialties in India). It also serves as a tertiary care referral centre.Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Admissions for MBBS and BDS programs are provided based on score/merit in NEET UG followed by counselling. MD/MS and Diploma admissions at Maulana Azad Medical College are based on the merit in NEET PG conducted by NBE.
    Admissions to M.Ch in Paediatric Surgery is based on merit in NEET SS followed by counselling.

  4. SANJAY GANDHI POSTGRADUATE INSTITUTE OF MEDICAL SCIENCES

    A medical institute recognised by the State Legislature Act, the Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences is located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, on Raebareli Road, 14 kilometres (8.7 mi) south of Hazratganj. It was founded in 1983 and bears Sanjay Gandhi's name. The Medical Council of India-approved degrees are available from this institution. It offers specialty-specific instruction, training, research, and tertiary medical treatment. The university grants DM, MCh, MD, and Ph.D. degrees in addition to postdoctoral fellowships and postdoctoral certificate programmes in a variety of subject areas. offers older citizens training programmes. The institution is self-sufficient. The institute runs as a state college, is accredited by the College Grants Commission, a member of the Association of Indian Universities. Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Candidates must obtain a valid score in the NEET SS exam to secure admission to super specialty courses such as DM and MCh at Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow. The final admission is based on further counselling rounds.

  5. UNIVERSITY COLLEGE OF MEDICAL SCIENCES

    University College of Medical Sciences (UCMS), a government college connected to the University of Delhi in New Delhi, was founded in 1971. In the fields of Medicine And Allied Sciences and Sciences, UCMS Delhi offers 27 courses. Courses at UCMS Delhi are available full-time. B.Sc. Medical Technology Radiography, M.B.B.S., M.D. Anaesthesiology, M.D. Forensic Medicine and Toxicology, M.D. Pharmacology, and many other courses are available at the University College of Medical Sciences. Students are offered admission to UCMS Delhi depending on how well they performed in the entrance exam (NEET/CUET/NEET PG), which is followed by the counselling process. The UCMS Delhi has up-to-date, contemporary facilities.Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Candidates must apply for NEET UG conducted by NTA to take admission to MBBS.
    For MD/MS programmes, admission will be based on the qualifying marks obtained in NEET PG.
    For admission to MDS programme, candidates must have a valid score in NEET MDS.

  6. INSTITUTE OF MEDICAL SCIENCES BHU,VARANASI


    7. The Institute Of Medical Sciences (IMS-BHU) is one of the Six institutes of Banaras Hindu University in Varanasi, India and comprises three faculties, Faculty of Medicine, Dental Sciences, and Ayurveda. The Institute of Medical Sciences is the medical school of the university. Like other institutions forming BHU, it is residential and co-educational Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Admission to MBBS/ BDS courses is through NEET UG Examinations.
    The counselling for MBBS/BDS courses will be conducted by Medical Counseling Committee (MCC).

  7. KING GEORGE MEDICAL UNIVERSITY


    8. King George's Medical University is a medical school, hospital, and medical university located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. The medical school was raised to a medical university by an act passed by the government of Uttar Pradesh on 16 September 2002. It is the only government medical institution to have been awarded the NAAC A+ grade in the state of Uttar Pradesh.The university has about 1250 undergraduate students (including 280 dental students) and 450 postgraduate students.About 250 students a year are admitted to the four-and-a-half-year course of study for the degree of M.B.B.S.Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    MBBS and BDS admissions are done only through NEET-UG.
    KGMU offers PG courses such as MD, MDS, DM, etc. Admission to these courses is entrance-based.

  8. CHRISTIAN MEDICAL COLLEGE,LUDHIANA


    9. Christian Medical College, Ludhiana was established by the minority Christian community in the year 1999 as a Christian Educational and Research Institution. The college is administered by the Christian Medical College Ludhiana Society (regd.) of Christian Churches and Christian agencies. The institute is affiliated to Baba Farid University of Health Sciences, Faridkot, Punjab, Ludhiana. Christian Medical College, Ludhiana offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses to the students. The college was ranked 18 by the NIRF for the year 2020 in the medical category. Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Admission to MBBS and BDS courses is based on the merit scored in NEET-UG.
    Applicants shall qualify NEET-PG for admission to MD/MS programs.
    Admission to the MDS course is offered to candidates qualifying in the NEET MDS examination.
    CMC Ludhiana also conducts admission tests for various UG and PG courses.

  9. ARMY COLLEGE OF MEDICAL SCIENCES


    10. Army College of Medical Sciences (also known as ACMS New Delhi) is an Indian medical college affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. It is supported by Army Welfare Education Society (AWES) of Indian Army for wards of serving and retired personnel of the Indian Army. It is one of the most prestigious college of Indian Army and is amongst top five emerging medical colleges of India . It is situated near Base Hospital Delhi Cantt, New Delhi. The annual intake of students to this college is 100. Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    Candidates must apply for NEET UG examination conducted by MCI to take admission in MBBS programme.

  10. VARDHMAN MAHAVIR MEDICAL COLLEGE


    11. Vardhman Mahavir Medical College (VMCC) is situated at Safdarjung Hospital. The college was established in November 2001 by the Government of India. VMMC comes under the Central Government, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. It is affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University and has recognition from Medical Council of India (MCI). The college offers MBBS, MS/MD and Super Speciality Courses. The institute has secured 18th rank in the Medical category in NIRF 2021 Ranking with an overall score of 56.2 out of 100. It had ranked 16th in the same category in NIRF 2020 Ranking. Official website
    ELIGIBILITY:
    VMMC offers MBBS and B.Sc (Hons) Nursing at the undergraduate level on the basis of NEET UG scores.
    At the postgraduate level, the college offers M.D. and M.S. courses in different specializations on the basis of NEET PG.
    VMMC also offers D.M, and M.Ch courses at the Super Specialty level based on NEET SS scores.
    The candidates need to appear for counseling based on NEET scores in order to secure admission to the college.
    The counseling for admission to VMMC under All-India Quota seats is conducted by Medical Counseling Committee.
    GGSIPU is responsible for conducting counseling for admission to VMMC under State-Quota seats.
