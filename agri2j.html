TOP AGRICULTURE COLLEGES IN SOUTH INDIA

  1. TAMILNADU AGRICULTURAL UNIVERSITY


    2. Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) is an agricultural university located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India and was established in 1971. The university is recognized by UGC and functions in accordance with Tamil Nadu Directorate of Education. In 1920, the college was affiliated to the University of Madras with the introduction of a three-year degree program. Till 1946, the Agricultural College and Research Institute, Coimbatore was the only institute for agricultural education for the whole of South India, It was established in 1971. TNAU has 36 Research Centers for agrotechnology development and 14 Farm Science Centers for outreach .The University is offering 13 Undergraduate Degree Programs, 29 Postgraduate Degree Programs and 26 Doctoral Programs in 14 Colleges distributed in 11 campuses all over Tamil Nadu. Official website

    ELIGIBILITY:
    For B.Tech and B.Sc admissions candidates must have passed in 10+2 examinations in relevant subjects with 55% aggregate. Diploma admissions are based on scores obtained in 10+2 exams.
    For admission to M.Tech, M.Sc & MBA courses, Bachelor's degree from TNAU recognized universities is required. Along with scores in the internal entrance.

  2. VELLORE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY,AGRICULTURE


    3. Vellore Institute of Technology also known as VIT University is deemed University one of the most sought-after private engineering universities in India. VIT University was founded in the year 1984 by the name Vellore Engineering College under Section 3 of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act, 1956. Later in the year 2001, the institute was granted University status by the UGC (University Grants Commission). At the initial stages, VIT only provided technical education programmes to the students, but later when the institute got University status, other undergraduate and post-graduate programmes were also started. Currently, VIT offers 19 graduate programs34 postgraduate programs, 2 integrated programs, 4 doctoral and research programs in Applied Sciences, Engineering, and Management. The 4-year B.Tech degree at the UG level, which is offered across 24 different specialisations as well as minor specialisations, is the most popular and most opted programme at VIT Vellore. Official website

    ELIGIBILITY:
    Admission to the B.Tech programme is done through the VITEEE entrance test, and to be eligible for admission, a candidate must have passed their 10+2 level board examination with 60% marks.
